Module 6: Equation extractor
Detection:

Find equations: Identify inline and display math (e.g., LaTeX markers, numbered equations).

Canonical capture: Store each equation in LaTeX exactly as written.

Annotation:

Equation role: Provide a short explanation (≤40 words) of the equation’s purpose and how it supports methodology or analysis, derived strictly from the surrounding text.

Linking: Map equations to their source section and paragraph/snippet.

Output:

Equation list: Ordered by section, with LaTeX, role, and location tag.
